LESSONS TO BE LEARNT

The contents taught will help you both physically and mentally and make you aware of all the hazards that you might face, Being fully prepared will give you the confidence to survive against the odds and build team spirit, and allows you to adapt when things go wrong.

THE ART OF SURVIVAL

The instinct to survive will never change, neither with the human body's amazing ability to endure. Yet we should also bear in mind that as we become more civilized, survival skills are being forgotten. It is therefore more important which each passing day to make it count to practice the survival skills and be prepared for anything. Survival training is the best Insurance policy you can take in an unstable world. You could be isolated anywhere in the world from the arctic to a desert, from a tropical rainforest to an open ocean. Each environment calls for special techniques, Mountains, Jungles, Open Plains and Swamps can seem hazardous to the survivor but each offer some form of support and can be exploited for food, fuel, water, shelter if you know how to survive.

Survival is above all a mental exercise. What keeps us going is the basic instinct, as "will to live".
